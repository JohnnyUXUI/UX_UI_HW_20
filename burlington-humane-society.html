UX HYPOTHESIS


THE PROBLEM:


  • •Burlington Humane Society does not provide sufficient information about the animals which makes the adoption process more difficult.
  • •The adoption process is not clear enough for users.
  • •Information on web page is disorganized and overwhelming.
  • •Causing users to not have confidence in their animal adoption of choice.



THE SOLUTION:

We believe that by organizing the information effectively and by clearly outlining the adoption process for our users, this will help us reach our objective of finding a suitable pet for interested adopters.
